About Turtles

Turtles are fascinating creatures known for their hard shells and slow movements. They belong to the order Testudines and are found in various environments, including oceans, freshwater habitats, and land. Turtles have been around for over 200 million years, making them one of the oldest reptile groups.

Interesting Facts

  • Turtles can live for over 100 years.
  • They have a special respiratory system that allows them to hold their breath for extended periods.
  • The shell of a turtle is made up of over 50 bones and is an integral part of its skeleton.
  • Some species of turtles can retract their head and limbs into their shell for protection.
